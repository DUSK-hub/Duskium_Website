About DUSK

I'm Yousef, but you can call me DUSK. I'm a second-year Computer Science student at a university in Cairo, Egypt, with a unique blend of technical expertise and physical discipline.

My Journey

My path into tech started at age 8 when I wanted a paid game but had no money - so I started learning how to hack. That curiosity turned into an 11-year journey through computers, and now I'm pursuing formal education in Computer Science.

For the past 2 years, I've worked as an IT Manager at various companies, solving problems and managing systems. But what makes me different is my approach: I don't just fix problems, I brainstorm solutions that make sense.

Beyond the Screen

When I'm not coding, you'll find me in the gym doing calisthenics, training MMA, riding my bike, or drifting and racing cars. I believe physical discipline sharpens mental clarity - the same focus that helps me land a punch helps me debug code.

My approach is simple: fast learning, deep thinking, real solutions. People's support motivates me, and I tackle any problem that comes my way with my greatest tool - my mind.
